Rules and FAQ's

  • Rules

  • Enter 5 letter words using the device keyboard or the onscreen keyboard provided on the website.
  • You will be given 6 tries to guess the hidden word.
  • If the word that you have entered does not exist in the wordle's dictionary, then it will not get accepted.
  • Each letter of the word that gets accepted will be coloured in either green, yellow or grey.
  • If you are able to correcty guess the word within 6 tries, you win.

  • Green

  • If a letter in the word that you entered is coloured green, it means that in the hidden word, this letter occurs in the same position.

  • Yellow

  • If a letter in the word that you entered is coloured yellow, it means that this letter occurs in the hidden word but not in the same position.

  • Grey

  • If a letter in the word that you entered is coloured grey, it means that this letter does not occur in the hidden word at all.

  • General FAQ

  • When to play?
    • The hidden word in our wordle updates every day. You can visit us every 24 hours to play and find out the word of the day!
  • How many attempts do I get to guess the word?
    • You get 6 attempts to correctly guess the hidden word. The fewer you take the better.
  • What does the green background on a letter mean?
    • It means that letter occurs in the same position in the hidden word as well.
  • What does the yellow backgorund on a letter mean?
    • It means that the letter occurs in the hidden word but not in the same position.
  • What does the grey background on a letter mean?
    • It means that letter does not occur in the hidden word.
  • How to start a new game?
    • Just click on the "Wordle" on the top left of your screen and enjoy your new game!
